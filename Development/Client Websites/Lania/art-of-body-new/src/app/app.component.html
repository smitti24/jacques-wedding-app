For the past few years I have worked as a physiotherapist in various private- and public health settings in South Africa. I also conducted Pilates classes to groups and on a one-on-one basis. I fell in love with Pilates when I saw what a great effect it has on individuals. The Pilates method revolves around improving your posture, strengthening and lengthening your muscles, and balancing your body and mind. The basis of Pilates training is your powerhouse (abdominal, back and pelvic floor muscles). By training your powerhouse, you improve your stability. Your body becomes stronger and more balanced and you have control over your own body.
